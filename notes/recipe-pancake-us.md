American Pancake
================

Ingredients
-----------

-   135g flour
-   1 tsp baking powder
-   1/2 tsp salt
-   2 tbsp sugar
-   1 teaspoon baking soda
-   1 large egg or 2 medium
-   50g melted butter
-   130ml milk

Method
------

- start with max setting on the pancake maker, then decrease
- 3 minutes, 1cm thick, golden brown
- 5x 10-15 cm diameter
