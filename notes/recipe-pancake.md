Pancake
=======

Ingredients
-----------

-   300 g flour
-   2-3 eggs
-   700ml milk, but start small
-   oil

Anya

-   20 dkg liszt
-   2 tojas
-   750 ml tej
-   10 dkg cukor
-   szoda: tej fele
-   csipet so

Method
------

Mix the flour with the eggs and as little milk as possible.
~10-15 pancakes
