Bottom bracket
--------------

- threaded
- frame inner diameter
  - size?
- bracket width

  - measure the inside of the bottom bracket shell in your frame
  - 73mm, 70mm or **68mm**
- spindle length
  - determined by the crankset
  - look up specification **113mm** (?)
  - or measure original **121mm** (???)
